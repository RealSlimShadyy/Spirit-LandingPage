What is SPIRIT?

Spirit is the annual sports festival of IIT Guwahati, one of the largest sports festivals in North-east India. Being held from 3 to 5 November, it provides a platform for all budding sportspersons to showcase their talent and engage in various sports activities. It allows players all over the country to iron out the flaws in their game and gain somevaluable match practice!
